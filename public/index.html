Learn how to track your emotional state

Welcome to Flow-State! Here we are trying to understand how to track our emotional state to try and better learn how our emotions affect our daily performance. Ultimately Flow-State was created to give us a bit of insight about ourselves and how we are feeling.

Please read carefully how this works

When you go to the next page and upload a video of yourself understand that the info used is sent to a third party company for analysis. That third party is Kairos. That video is stored on our protected server for no more than 30 or 40 seconds. You will need to register with F-S to begin.
