Track your emotional state

Welcome to Flow-State! Here we are trying to understand how to track our emotional state to try and better learn how our emotions affect our daily performance. We recommend taking several videos throughout the day and keeping a notepad log on how much work you got done in between videos.

Please read carefully how this works

When you go to the next page and upload a video of yourself understand that the video is analyzed through a third party immediately and deleted after. That third party is Kairos. That video is stored on our protected server for no more than 30 or 40 seconds.
